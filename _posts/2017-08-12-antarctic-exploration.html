See our airborne gravity meter AT1A-5 as it flies above the Antarctic Ross Ice Shelf aboard one of the New York Air National Guard's LC-130 planes.

AT1A-5 was manufactured for Columbia University's Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory (LDEO) Polar Geophysics group, where they have used the sensor to perform surveys of the Ross Ice Shelf.
