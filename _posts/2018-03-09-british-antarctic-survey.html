Dynamic Gravity Systems (DgS) announces a contract with the British Antarctic Survey (part of the National Environmental Research Council), to provide a new Advanced Technology Marine Gravity Meter (AT1M) for their new research vessel, the RSS Sir David Attenborough.

The RSS Sir David Attenborough is expected to be complete construction and outfitting in 2018, to be launched in 2019.
