--- title: System Repair & Control Module Upgrades pos: left subject: RFQ Repair Services active: true ---
  • DgS can offer repair and upgrade services on Micro-g Lacoste (MgL) AirSeaII systems, both air-damped and magnetically damped.
  • We also manufacture and sell new control modules for MgL AirSeaII systems which can be field-swapped in place of the original systems. These modules have vastly updated and more robust computer data acquisition systems, as well as an updated software interface.
  • DgS also offers diagnostics and repair services, we can travel to your facility, or to you in the field - worldwide, to troubleshoot and diagnose your AirSeaII systems.
  • Depending on the issue and diagnosis we may be able to effect field repairs on your system, or for more complex problems we can perform a full factory servicing at our offices.
